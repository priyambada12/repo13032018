Bengaluru

An Overview

The capital city of Karnataka, Bengaluru is globally known as India’s Silicon Valley. The city, a major information technology hub, has wide roads and a lot of greenery. Bengaluru’s pleasant climate invites immigrants in great numbers. A large of MNCs and immigrants drive the city’s rental market in a big way.
